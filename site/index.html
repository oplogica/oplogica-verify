How it works

From decision record to reviewable export

A deterministic pipeline. Every stage is recomputed from the signed record — no model interpretation, no free-text claim extraction.

1AI Decision RecordThe recorded inputs, reasoning, and outcome of a decision.
2Evidence BundleStructured, signed evidence bound to a governance registry.
3Deterministic VerificationIntegrity, binding, and tamper-evidence checks recomputed from the bundle.
4L3 Failure TaxonomyStructural failures classified by kind, with explicit scope limits.
5Reviewable ExportA reconciled, human-reviewable record of what was — and was not — established.
Capabilities

What Oplogica verifies

Deterministic, structural checks over the recorded evidence — precise about what each one establishes.

Evidence integrity

Verifies recorded artifact integrity so the bundle cannot be silently altered after generation.

Tamper detection

Recomputes hashes and signatures to flag any post-generation change to the recorded evidence.

Registry binding

Confirms the evidence binds to an authentic governance registry under a pinned trust root.

L3 failure taxonomy

Flags structural evidence failures and classifies them by kind, deterministically.

Negative claims firewall

Keeps overclaim boundaries visible by guarding Oplogica's own generated output.

Verification discipline

Publishes a machine-readable posture: deterministic, no LLM interpretation, scope made explicit.

Scope & honesty

What Oplogica does not claim

Honest scope is the product. Oplogica verifies evidence integrity and binding — and is explicit about the limits of that guarantee.

Integrity and binding — verified. Everything else — out of scope, by design.
